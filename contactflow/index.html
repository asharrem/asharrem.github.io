Recent Contacts Actions

Every call and message in your communication feed includes these powerful actions:

Map

Choose from contact addresses and open in your preferred map application. Disabled when no addresses are available.

Call

Present a list of contact phone numbers and call directly using your device's phone app.

SMS ETA

Send estimated travel time from your current location to any of the contact's addresses via SMS.

Log Interaction

Create a calendar event with intelligent titles like "Call with John Doe" or "SMS with +1234567890".

Create Event

Open your native calendar app with a pre-filled "Follow-up" event ready to schedule.

Open/Create Contact

Access or create contact records directly in your device's native contacts app.

Add Location

Copy your current location to clipboard and open the contact for easy address addition.

Calendar Events Actions

Every event in your calendar includes these context-aware actions:

Map

Open the event location in your preferred map application for navigation or exploration.

Call

Call event attendees directly using their phone numbers from your contact list.

SMS ETA

Send travel time estimates to event attendees from your current location.

Create Event

Create follow-up events that duplicate all original details and schedule for the next day.

Open/Create Contact

Access or create contact records for event attendees in your native contacts app.

Update Location

Copy your current location and update the event with the new address information.
